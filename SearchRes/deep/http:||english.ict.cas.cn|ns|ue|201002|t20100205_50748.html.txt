 Upcoming Events:   Resource Allocation in 4G Wireless Data Networks
 Upcoming Events:   Network Routing for Efficient Energy Usage
Time: pm3:00 February 4,2010Place: Meeting Hall, 4th Floor, ICT, CASSpeaker: Prof. David Du, University of Minnesota 
Abstract: This talk will examine the challenges in the convergence of both network centric and data centric computing, and present a vision of a content addressable future Internet. It will also present a number of research projects that are carried out in our Intelligent Storage Consortium. These projects include data deduplication, long-term data preservation, data/storage security/privacy, and solid state drives. 
Bio:Dr. Du is an IEEE Fellow (since 1998) and a Fellow of the Minnesota Supercomputer Institute. He received a Ph.D. degree from University of Washington (Seattle), a M.S from University of Washington and a B.S. from National Tsing-Hua University (Taiwan) in 1981, 1980 and 1974 respectively. He joined University of Minnesota as a faculty since 1981.
