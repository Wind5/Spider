Jiang Xiaoye, the Director of Storage Platforms of EMC China, visited ICT for exploring cooperation opportunity with his delegation on June 16, 2011. They gave students a lecture “Cloud Meets Big Data” and had a seminar with ICT researchers from Storage Group, Distributed System Group and High Performance Computing Group. They also discussed the possibility of ICT students’ internship at EMC. EMC is a global leading company in enabling businesses and service providers to transform their operations and deliver Information Technology as a service.
