"Innovation Unlimited" section in CCTV Science Channel 10's, has presented an ad hoc program on the special effects shooting robot "OTHKA" on May 30. "OTHKA" is a control system for camera motion. It is invented by researchers in Virtual Reality Group of ICT, CAS. 
The CCTV program gave an explicit illustration on the role of "OTHKA" played in special effects film shooting stage. Moreover, it showed on site how "OTHKA" could produce a magic clip of "one-man band" very easily and quickly.
