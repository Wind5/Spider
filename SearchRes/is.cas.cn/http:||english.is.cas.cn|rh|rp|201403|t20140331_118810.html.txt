 Service Release Conference of Public Technology Service Platform was held 
 Service Release Conference of Public Technology Service Platform was held on October 25th, 2012. ”Internet of Things Software Development Test and Evaluation Platform” designed by Wuxi Branch, ISCAS, is one of three public technology service platforms released on this conference. It was showed officially on the 3rd China International Internet of Things (sensor network) Expo. In the release conference, the Wuxi Branch of ISCAS signed Cooperation Agreement of Platform Application Service with other 7 companies and centers.
