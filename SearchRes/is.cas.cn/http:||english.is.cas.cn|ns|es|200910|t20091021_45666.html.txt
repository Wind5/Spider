 The Unveiling Ceremony of China Software Technology Museum was held 
 The unveiling ceremony of China Software Technology Museum(CSTM) was held on October 16, 2009, at ISCAS. CSTM hosted by ISCAS is the first museum which focuses on software technology in China.
