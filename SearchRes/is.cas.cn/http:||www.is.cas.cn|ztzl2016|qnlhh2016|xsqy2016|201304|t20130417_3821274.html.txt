The advent of big data is greatly transforming many fields of science. It also presents opportunities and challenges to the communities of statisticians and computer scientists.  I will discuss some current statistical and computational methods for analyzing big data and visualizing the results. Throughout the talk, methods will be demonstrated with applications to large-scale genomics and metagenomics data.　 
　  Big Data:Computational and Statistical opportunities and challenges
