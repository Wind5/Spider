 Two scientific project has been granted the Second-level of National Science and Technology Progress Awards 
 The scientific project “Open Network Computing Platform (ONCE):Technology and Systems” undertaken by Institute of software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS) has been granted the Second-level of National Science and Technology Progress Awards. Mr. Chunli Bai, the president of CAS, sent a letter for congratulations on February, 2014.
