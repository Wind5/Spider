 The big data open source community meetup was held 
 In collaboration with the EasyHadoop community, ISCAS successfully held the big data open source community meetup at the lecture hall on Nov 24, 2012. There were more than 260 attendees from over 100 companies, research institutes and universities, which covered the vast majority of well-know IT companies and related organizations in China. There was also a remote venue at Guangzhou by webcast. A pioneer Internet streaming media TV broadcaster named UUSee did the live broadcast for the whole event online.
